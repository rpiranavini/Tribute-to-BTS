Tribute to Kim Taehyung

Kim Tae-hyung also known professionally as V, is a South Korean singer and member of the boy band BTS. He was born in 1995 December 30.Since his debut with the group in 2013, V has released three solo tracks under their name—"Stigma" in 2016, "Singularity" in 2018, and "Inner Child" in 2020—all of which charted on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart. He appeared on the soundtrack for the television series Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth in 2016 and released his first independent song, the self-composed "Scenery", in 2019.
